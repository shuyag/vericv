VeriCV allows you to verify your graduate's credentials.

Millions of hard-working students earn diplomas and certificates from traditional and online schools but have no way to easily prove their achievements. Meanwhile, thousands of people distort or lie on their résumés and LinkedIn profiles and honest people have no recourse.

Now graduates can prove their success with VeriCV.

SIMPLE
TO USE 

Integrating with our API takes just a few minutes of developer time. Once set-up, the degree verification process will be an automated process which can be done once per semester.

REWARD YOUR
STUDENTS 

In just a few clicks, your students can add their diploma stamps™ to their C.V. and LinkedIn pages. The diploma stamps™ are discreet while making a clear statement.

PROTECT YOUR REPUTATION

Audits of C.V.s and LinkedIn pages show that thousands of people are misusing your school’s name. These are not the people you want associated with your community and brand. Prevent misuse once and for all.
